ESCAPE STEREOTYPING IN A PLAYFUL WAY

Samen bouwen aan een
toekomst zonder stereotypes

Project Boxicity

“Education is the most powerful weapon
which you can use to change the world.”

Problem
Prejudice is very common in the society we live in. The judgements that are made about someone or something often happen unconsciously and in advance. This can lead to discrimination against a person or a group of people. In project Boxicity we want to tackle the negative connotations linked to a stereotype. Let us break together the toxic practice of putting people into boxes!


Mission
Our mission is to create awareness about labelling among young adults aged 16 to 24. The aim is to get people to reflect on whether they themselves label groups within society and to understand why this is wrong. Furthermore, we want to give an experience of what it is like to be someone else in society.
A change in mindset around stereotypes is what we are working towards.



Intended Result
In order to create this awareness, we have created a board game based on a lot of research. By playing this game, the players encounter real-life situations through technological experiences such as virtual reality and interactive film stories. Moreover, the game contains other minigames and cards with questions that allow players to reflect and learn in a playful way.
