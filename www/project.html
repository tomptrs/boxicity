Plan International

Founded in 1937, Plan International is a development and humanitarian organisation that advances children’s rights and equality for girls. The non-governmental organisation strives for a just world, working together with children, young people, supporters, and partners. Plan International programs deliver lasting change against discriminatory norms, policies, and laws. In 2021 Plan works in 77 countries across the world and committed to making a lasting impact on the lives of the most vulnerable and excluded children.

Our approach

Basic info about the project
The gamification theory in education means that learners learn best when they are also having fun. That is why in our board game we combined the ME-YOU-US approach from Plan International with FEEL-ACT-LEARN methodology developed by our educators’ team. It is important to start with the individual feelings. This way you can make the person enthusiastic about the game itself. Only after the emotional connection is established, participants are motivated to feel, interact, and learn about the subject. Through the usage of immersive media such as Virtual Reality, we want to let people feel how it is to be a labelled person by putting him/her in the place of a labelled girl. During the interactive videos, we want to allow (inter)act in the situation of stereotyping or labelling. Besides that, participants will learn new information on the topics.
